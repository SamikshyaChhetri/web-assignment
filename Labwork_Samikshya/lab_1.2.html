Department of CSIT

About Us

The Department of Computer Science and Information Technology (CSIT) is dedicated to advancing knowledge and education in the fields of computing,
software engineering, and information systems. It focuses on preparing students with theoretical foundations and practical skills necessary to tackle real-
world challenges in technology-driven environments. The department emphasizes innovation, research, and interdisciplinary collaboration to meet the
evolving demands of the digital age

Key Features

  • Research and practical training.
  • Experienced faculty members specializing in various fields.
  • Collaborative projects with industry partners.

Academic Programs

  • Bachelor's Degree in Bsc.CSIT

Infrastructure of our department

  1. Computer Laboratories with modern computers and software for programming, simulations, and data analysis.
  2. Access to digital libraries, online journals, and specialized resources related to computer science and IT
  3. Spaces for academic discussions, seminars, and workshops.
  4. Technical support staff and help desks for assisting students and faculty with IT issues.
  5. Measures to ensure the security of data, equipment, and facilities.
